Wealth Inequality

Share of top 1% - Individual net wealth

Individual net wealth share held by top percential. A single source series is used, taken from WID.world. According to the WID's description of the dataset available in the sources and information tab, "the population is comprised of individuals over age 20. The base unit is the individual (rather than the household) but resources are split equally within couples". More details on the construction of this data is available in Chatterjee, Czajka & Gethin (2021) and Blanchet, Martinez-Toledano (2021).
