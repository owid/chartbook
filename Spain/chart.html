Top Income Shares

Share of top 1% - Pre-tax national income (equal-split adults)

Pre-tax national income share held by top percentile. A single source series is used, taken from WID.world. This data relates to individuals, assuming resources are split equally within couples.

Poverty

Share below 60% median - Equivalised disposable household income

Share of population with equivalised disposable household cash income below 60% of the median from EU-SILC (t2020_52 series), as reported by Eurostat; linked back at 1995 to series from Cantó (2003), based on the Continuous Surveys of Family Budgets (ECPF).

Wealth Inequality

Share of top 1% - Individual net wealth

Share of top 1 per cent in total individual wealth. A single source series is used, taken from Alvaredo and Blanco (forthcoming) based on inheritance tax data. For estimates based on the investment income method, see Blanchet & Martínez-Toledano (2021)
