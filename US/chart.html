Overall Income Inequality

Gini coefficient - Equivalised gross household income

Gini coefficient for gross equivalised household income from the U.S. Census Bureau (2020); linked at 2019 to series from the U.S. Census Bureau (2018); linked forward at 2017 to series from the U.S. Census Bureau (2016) due to a new methodology; linked forward at 2013 to series from the same source due to a new methodology; we assume that half of the recorded change between 1992 and 1993 was due to the methodological change, and therefore added 1.15 percentage points to the values from 1992 back to 1967; linked backwards at 1967 to series from Budd (1970) related to money income before tax for consumer units (families plus unrelated individuals); linked back at 1944 to the BEA synthetic series for gross family incomes, based on calculations from the tabulations in Brandolini (2002); linked back at 1929 to NBER/Brookings synthetic series for gross income of income recipients, based on calculations from the tabulations in Mitchell et al (1921) and Leven, Moulton and Warburton (1934).
