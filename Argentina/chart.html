Poverty

Share below 50% median - Household per capita income

Percentage of individuals below 50 per cent of median household per capita income from SEDLAC (2020) EPHC data (averages where multiple observations per year), for 31 main cities since the second half of 2006 and 28 main cities previously. We extend the series by linking backwards at 2003 to a previous vintage of SEDLAC (2016) which contains earlier observations. Within the 2016 release, we link backwards at 1998 to data for 15 main cities, and again at 1992 to data for Greater Buenos Aires.
